Pizza Rolls

Description

Once again, I'm sure you've tried pizza rolls before, but if you're anything like me you've become super lazy when it comes to food.

This recipe teaches you how to make the crispiest, most acceptable pizza rolls that ever existed. While this recipe takes longer than the microwave method, it sure is worth it.

Ingredients

  • Pizza rolls
  • Oven or toaster oven
  • Sauces of your choice

Steps

  1. First preheat your oven to a chill 450 degrees farenheit. We're looking to cook these morsels as quickly as possible in order to get prime crunch
  2. Place your rolls on an oven save rack or plate (I like to use a cookie sheet) and place in the oven once thouroughly preheated
  3. Cook for 5-7 minutes in a toaster oven, then flip and cook for another 5
  4. If you're using a regular oven, cook for 12 minutes and then remove them and enjoy
  5. Once your pizza rolls have cooled sufficiently, place your sauces of choice on your plate, and eat with chopsticks to avoid messy fingers
  6. Voila! You have just bit into your first restaurant quality pizza roll. Now you can never go back...
