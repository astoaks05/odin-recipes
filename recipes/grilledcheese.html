Grilled Cheese

Description

In this recipe you'll learn how to make a fucking banger grilled cheese to eat when you're smacked out of your mind

With only a few ingredients and a short cook time, this recipe is the perfect midnight snack, or hearty lunch to get you through the rest of your day!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese of your choice (sliced)
  • Butter
  • Mayonaisse

Steps

  1. In a medium sized frying pan melt one tablespoon of butter
  2. While butter is melting, cover the outside of two pieces of bread with mayonaisse
  3. Place the first piece of bread mayo side down, and place 2-4 pieces of cheese on the plain side
  4. Next, place your other piece of bread mayo side up, and cover your pan with the lid
  5. Let it sit for around 5 minutes on low heat, or just until cheese is melted
  6. Once cheese is melted, turn up your heat to high and toast each side until golden brown
